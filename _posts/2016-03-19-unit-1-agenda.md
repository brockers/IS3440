### Stages/Categories for Hacking Tools

* Data Acquisition & Footprinting
	* **Examples:** Google, ipconfig * mitre-CVE
* Active Scanning
	* **Example:** nmap, ping
* System Hacking
	* **Example:** cisco-auditing-tool, Aircrack-ng
* Privilege Escalation
	* **Example:** rootkits
* Backdoors
	* **Example:** Netcat
* Clean-up & Cover Tracks
	* **Example:** screen, tmux
